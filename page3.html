Tips For Your Pics

Getting the most out of your FlawlessFoto experience.

#1

Looking dull?

This is a simple fix. Make sure to adjust the photo's saturation to alter the intensity of color in your image. This should brighten things up a bit.

#2

Brighter isn't always better.

Making your photos super bright isn't always the answer. Sometimes, turning down the brightness will actually give better results by making the image less foggy.

#3

Want something sharper?

Be bold and turn that grayscale all the way up. Having a fully black and white photo is often exactly what people need to add some dimensions and depth to their photos.

#4

Feeling odd?

It's probably the orientation. Give inverting your image a try (especially selfies) to get the most comfortable and symmetrical results.
